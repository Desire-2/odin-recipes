Ingredients

  • Chicken (whole or pieces)<\
  • Beef (ground or stew meat)
  • Pork (ribs or chops)
  • Fish (catfish or tilapia)
  • Shrimp
  • Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Dip the chicken pieces in buttermilk, then coat them in the cornmeal mixture.
  4. Place the coated chicken on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and bell peppers. Sauté until they become soft and fragrant.
  6. Add the beef, pork, or shrimp to the pot and cook until browned.
  7. Stir in the tomatoes, okra, and seasonings (paprika, cayenne pepper, thyme, bay leaves, cumin, and oregano).
  8. Add water or broth as needed, cover the pot, and let the mixture simmer for about an hour.
  9. While the meat and vegetables simmer, prepare the collard greens by removing the stems and cutting them into strips.
  10. In a separate pot, cook the collard greens with bacon or smoked sausage for added flavor. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Prepare the sweet potatoes by peeling and cutting them into chunks. Boil or roast until tender.
  12. Cook the black-eyed peas according to package instructions.
  13. Prepare the rice according to package instructions or using your preferred method.
  14. Serve the baked chicken, meat and vegetable stew, collard greens, sweet potatoes, black-eyed peas, and rice together as a delicious Juneteenth meal.

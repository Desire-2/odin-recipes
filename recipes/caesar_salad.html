Caesar Salad

Description

A classic Caesar salad recipe featuring crisp romaine lettuce, homemade croutons, Parmesan cheese, and a tangy Caesar dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce leaves. Tear them into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add the croutons to the bowl with the lettuce.
  3. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the salad.
  4. Pour Caesar dressing over the salad, starting with a small amount, and toss to coat the lettuce evenly. Add more dressing as desired.
  5. Season with black pepper to taste and toss again to combine.
  6. Serve the Caesar salad immediately as a side dish or as a main course by adding grilled chicken or shrimp, if desired.

Pasta Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish featuring spaghetti or linguine tossed with a creamy egg-based sauce, pancetta or bacon, and grated cheese.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti or linguine
  • 4 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish

    • Steps

    1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
    3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, and black pepper.
    4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the pancetta or bacon, tossing to combine.
    5. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring continuously to coat the pasta evenly.
    6. The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce. If needed, return the skillet to low heat to thicken the sauce slightly.
    7. Divide the pasta into serving plates or bowls and garnish with chopped parsley.
    8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
